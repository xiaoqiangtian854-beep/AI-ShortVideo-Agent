# Enterprise Promo Video Workflow

## Goal

Turn a company service, product, or event into a concise promotional video plan.

## Workflow

1. Define the viewer: customer, partner, recruit, or internal team.
2. Identify one business promise.
3. Collect proof: footage, screenshots, testimonials, numbers, or before-after examples.
4. Write a script with this structure:
   - Problem
   - Solution
   - Proof
   - Process
   - Call to action
5. Produce one main video and three cutdowns.

## Review Checks

- Is the promise specific?
- Is the proof visible?
- Can the video work without sound?
- Is the call to action clear?
